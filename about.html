About Me

Dr. Rishabh Das is an Assistant Professor at the Scripps College of Communication, Ohio University. With over a decade of hands-on experience in operating, troubleshooting, and supervising control systems in the oil and gas industry, Dr. Das has become a recognized leader in the field of cybersecurity.

His award-winning work in developing security architectures and detection algorithms has significantly advanced the protection of legacy industrial controllers against cyber threats. Dr. Das's research portfolio is extensive, encompassing the virtualization of Industrial Control Systems (ICS), threat modeling, penetration testing in ICS, active network monitoring, and the application of Machine Learning (ML) in cybersecurity.

Prior to joining Ohio University, Dr. Das served as an Advisory Specialist Master at Deloitte and Touche LLP, where he led cybersecurity initiatives for critical infrastructure across various industries. His expertise has been sought after by military associations and commercial entities alike, guiding them in meeting their cybersecurity requirements for critical infrastructure.

Dr. Das earned his Ph.D. in Computer Engineering from the University of Alabama in Huntsville, where his research focused on developing embedded defense-in-depth modules for detecting cyberattacks on interdependent SCADA controllers. His commitment to advancing the field of cybersecurity has resulted in numerous publications, presentations, and contributions to industry standards.
