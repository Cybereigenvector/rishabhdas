Talks & Presentations

I regularly share my research and expertise at prestigious conferences, workshops, and industry events worldwide. My presentations focus on advancing the understanding of critical infrastructure cybersecurity, industrial control systems protection, and emerging threats in operational technology environments. Through these talks, I aim to foster collaboration between academia and industry to develop robust security solutions for our essential systems.
